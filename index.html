Our History

Coffee Me Up was founded in 2010 by Sarah Collins and James Rivera in Portland, Maine. Coffee me up began as a small roastery offering fair-trade, organic coffee. Over time, it expanded to include sustainable coffee pods, cold brews, and specialty blends. Committed to eco-friendly practices like compostable packaging and carbon-neutral shipping, the company now operates globally, supporting reforestation and community projects in coffee-growing regions.
