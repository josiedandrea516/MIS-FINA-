Our History

Coffee Me Up was founded in 2010 by Sarah Collins and James Rivera in Portland, Maine. Originally a small roastery offering fair-trade coffee, it has grown into a global brand supporting sustainability initiatives worldwide.

Why Use Us

We offer premium eco-friendly coffee products while supporting local farmers and community development. Our commitment to sustainability, fair-trade sourcing, and customer satisfaction sets us apart.
